#how to add value in this DataModel file
#first word is the name of the variable
#second word is the category of the variable (action, subject,resource) and the identifier of the variable separated with a ";"
#third word is the type of the variable (string or integer or double)
#each word is separated with a ";"
#example :
#toto;subject-titi;string
