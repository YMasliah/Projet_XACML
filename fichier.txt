usage: XACML-SMT [-d] [-h] [-m <arg>] [-p <arg>] [-q <arg>] [-smt] [-u]
       [-v] [-xv <arg>] [-z3]
XACML SMT Analysis Tool V2

 -d          print policy variables and enum domains
 -h          print the help
 -m <arg>    number of models to list, [default 0]
 -p <arg>    list of policies (order is important)
 -q <arg>    query, [default `P_1']
 -smt        show SMT encoding of policies
 -u          show proof for UnSAT
 -v          verbose (lots of console output)
 -xv <arg>   XACML version; 2 for v2, 3 for v3, [default v3]
 -z3         show Z3 output and statistics
